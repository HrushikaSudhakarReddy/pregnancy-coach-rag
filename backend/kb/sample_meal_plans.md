# Sample Meal Templates
## 1-Day Template (Omnivore)
- **Breakfast:** Oatmeal with chia & berries; nut butter.
- **Snack:** Yogurt (or dairy-free) with fruit.
- **Lunch:** Whole-grain wrap with beans or chicken, veg, avocado.
- **Snack:** Hummus with carrots; small handful of nuts.
- **Dinner:** Salmon or tofu, quinoa, roasted vegetables.
- **Hydration:** ~8–10 cups water (adjust per provider guidance).

## 1-Day Template (Vegetarian/Vegan)
- **Breakfast:** Tofu scramble with spinach, whole-grain toast, fruit.
- **Snack:** Smoothie (banana, soy milk, peanut butter, oats).
- **Lunch:** Lentil & veggie bowl with brown rice and tahini.
- **Snack:** Apple with almond butter, or edamame.
- **Dinner:** Chickpea curry with quinoa; side salad.
