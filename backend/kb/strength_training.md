# Strength Training (Pregnancy-safe Patterns)
- Focus on compound, low-to-moderate load movements: squats to box, supported split squats, seated rows, cable/band pulls, elevated push-ups.
- Keep sets shorter (e.g., **2–3 sets of 8–12 reps**) with good form; long Valsalva holds are discouraged.
- Replace supine core work with **side-lying, quadruped, or tall-kneeling** variations.
- Emphasize **posture, breath, and pelvic-floor-friendly** strategies (exhale on exertion).
- Rest 60–90 seconds between sets; prioritize comfort and stability over load progression.
