# Yoga & Mobility
- Gentle flow and prenatal yoga are typically suitable; avoid deep closed twists, prolonged supine positions, and hot yoga.
- Prioritize **hip, thoracic, and ankle mobility** with slow, controlled ranges.
- Incorporate **cat-cow, child's pose, thread-the-needle, hip flexor openers**, and ankle rocks.
- Emphasize **slow breathing** and relaxation to support recovery and sleep.
